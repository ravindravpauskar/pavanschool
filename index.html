Learning Begins With Us

We, at Pavan School offer supportive and inspirational environments for young enquiring minds to learn and grow with us. Our passion for learning means we achieve more than outstanding results. We strive to build confident and creative thinkers and aim at delivering an education that is truly relevant to their future.

We are an early learning academy focused on social-emotional development and early literacy and numeracy. Our students walk out with the character and confidence to make their mark in the world, equipped with the knowledge and real-world skills that take them way ahead in the industry they may serve.

Our Core Features

The school has produced more than 7000 Alumni with over glorious 20 SSLC batches with continuously producing remarkable STATE RANKS year by year. Pavan foster over 200 qualified staff every year. PAVAN aims not just to impart knowledge to the students but also to inculcate in them wisdom, compassion, and humanitarian spirit. Discipline, values and integrity are the very foundation of this school. We believe- FOUNDATION will build big dreams; right nurture will build in confidence to make them come true. We are proud that our students have been constantly reaching to the highest levels of education, be it in IIT, NIT- Engineering, Civil Services like IAS and KAS, Medicine, Law, CA, CS and also to the top ranked Universities in the world.
